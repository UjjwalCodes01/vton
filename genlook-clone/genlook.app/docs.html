Welcome to Genlook

Genlook is a suite of apps built specifically for fashion brands. Whether you’re adding AI-powered virtual try-on to your Shopify store or integrating our standalone API into a custom platform, Genlook is designed to help you increase conversions, reduce returns, and build more engaging shopping experiences.

​
Products

Virtual Try-On

Add AI virtual try-on to your Shopify store. Let customers upload a photo to instantly see how your apparel looks on them. Install the app and go live in minutes, or build a bespoke integration.

API / Whitelabel (Developers)

Integrate virtual try-on into any e-commerce platform, mobile app, or custom storefront via a standalone REST API. Built for developers working outside of the Shopify ecosystem.

Shoppable Video & Reels

Turn your product videos into interactive, shoppable experiences. Let your customers browse and buy directly from your video content.

Size Chart

Help customers find the perfect fit with AI-powered size recommendations, reducing returns and boosting confidence at checkout.
