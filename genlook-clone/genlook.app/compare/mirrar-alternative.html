Hard Goods vs. Soft Goods

The difference between AR (Augmented Reality) and AI (Generative AI) comes down to what you are selling. Choosing the right tech stack is crucial.

MirrAR: The Rigid Specialist

MirrAR uses AR to overlay 3D models onto a video feed. This is perfect for rigid objects like rings, watches, and necklaces. These items don't change shape when you move.

When to choose MirrAR: If you sell jewelry or watches, MirrAR is the superior choice. The high-fidelity reflection and shine of metal/gems require 3D rendering that AI can't yet match perfectly.

Genlook: The Fabric Specialist

Clothing is "soft"—it bends, folds, and stretches. Traditional AR treats clothes like stiff plastic shells ("paper dolls"). Genlook uses Generative AI to understand how fabric behaves.

Why Genlook wins for Apparel: We hallucinate the garment onto the person, creating natural shadows and folds that adapt to the body's pose. This is impossible with standard AR without Hollywood-level physics simulations.

Why Merchants Choose Genlook

Clothing requires a different approach

AR is perfect for rigid items like rings or watches. But for clothing, you need technology that understands fabric, gravity, and body shape.

Eliminate 3D Costs

MirrAR requires high-fidelity 3D models for every SKU. This is standard for jewelry but prohibitively expensive for fast-fashion. Genlook uses your standard 2D photos to create 3D-like results instantly.

Soft-Body Physics

Genlook's AI hallucinates how fabric folds, stretches, and drapes over a human body. Simple AR overlays often look like "paper doll" cutouts when applied to clothing.

Start Immediately

No sales calls, no onboarding fees, no contracts. Install Genlook, upload a photo, and go live in minutes. We believe high-tech tools should be accessible to every brand.

If you are selling engagement rings or luxury watches, MirrAR is the gold standard. But if your catalog is apparel, Genlook offers a solution purpose-built for the complexities of fabric and fit, at a fraction of the cost.
