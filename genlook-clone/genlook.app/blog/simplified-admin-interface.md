At Genlook, we believe that powerful tools should be simple to use. After analyzing how merchants interact with our app and listening to your feedback, we've rolled out a significant update to our admin interface.

## The Shift to Collections

We noticed that **95% of our merchants** prefer to manage virtual try-on availability at the collection level rather than product by product. Managing settings for hundreds of individual products is tedious and time-consuming.

To address this, we have:

*   **Prioritized Collection Settings**: The dashboard now focuses on enabling Genlook for entire collections (e.g., "Dresses", "New Arrivals").
*   **Removed Granular Complexity**: We've removed the rarely used per-product settings that were cluttering the interface.
*   **Simplified the UI**: The new design is cleaner, faster, and more intuitive.

## What This Means for You

*   **Faster Setup**: Enable virtual try-on for hundreds of products in just a few clicks.
*   **Easier Maintenance**: When you add new products to a collection, they automatically inherit the virtual try-on settings if the collection is enabled.
*   **Less Confusion**: A clearer interface means less time figuring out settings and more time focusing on your business.

We've also fixed a bug related to enabling multiple collections at once, ensuring a smooth experience even for stores with complex catalog structures.

Check out the new simplified dashboard in your Shopify Admin today!
