We're excited to announce a major time-saving feature for our merchants: you can now enable the Genlook virtual try-on widget for entire product collections at once.

Previously, you had to activate Genlook on a product-by-product basis. While this offered granular control, it could be time-consuming for stores with large catalogs.

## How It Works

1.  **Go to your Genlook Dashboard:** Navigate to the "Product Settings" section.
2.  **Select "Collections":** You'll see a new option to manage collections.
3.  **Choose your collections:** Simply select the collections you want to enable Genlook for.
4.  **Save your changes:** The Genlook widget will now automatically appear on all product pages within the selected collections.

This new feature makes it much faster to launch virtual try-on across your store, so you can start boosting conversions and reducing returns even sooner.
