## Step 1: Install the Genlook App

The first step is getting the technology onto your platform.

1.  Navigate to the Shopify App Store and search for **Genlook**, or [click here to go directly to the listing](https://apps.shopify.com/genlook-virtual-try-on?utm_source=g_landing&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=blog-add-virtual-try-on-shopify-store).
2.  Click the **Install** button.
3.  Review the necessary permissions (we need access to read your product images to generate the try-on experience) and click **Approve**.
4.  You will be redirected to the Genlook dashboard within your Shopify Admin.

![Install Genlook on Shopify](/blog-images/genlook-shopify-listing.png)

## Step 2: Enable Genlook on Your Products

Once installed, Genlook needs to know which products should have the "Try-On" feature enabled. You have full control over this.

In the Genlook dashboard:
1.  Navigate to the **Products** or **Collections** tab.
2.  You can select individual items to enable, or select entire collections (e.g., "New Arrivals" or "Dresses").
3.  Toggle the status to **Enabled**.

**Pro Tip:** You don't need to enable your entire catalog on day one. We recommend starting with your highest-traffic items that suffer from lower conversion rates—these are the products where VTO makes the biggest immediate impact.

![Enable Genlook on your products](/blog-images/genlook-dashboard-products-tab.png)

## Step 3: Add and Configure the Button in Your Theme

Now that the backend is ready, you need to place the "Try-On" button on your product pages so customers can see it. Genlook is built for Shopify Online Store 2.0, making this drag-and-drop easy.

1.  From your Shopify Admin, go to **Online Store > Themes**.
2.  Click **Customize** on your current live theme.
3.  At the top center dropdown, select **Products** and then your **Default product** template.
4.  On the left-hand sidebar menu, look for the "Product Information" section. Click **+ Add Block**.
5.  Scroll down to the "Apps" section and select the **Genlook Try-On Button**.
6.  **Position it:** Drag the block up so it sits right below your "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now" button for maximum visibility.
7.  **Style it:** Click on the Genlook block itself. On the right-hand menu, you can now customize the button text (e.g., "Try On," "See it on Me"), background colors, and text colors to perfectly match your brand identity.

![Add and configure the button in your theme](/blog-images/genlook-theme-editor.png)

## Step 4: Test the Experience Live

Never assume it works until you try it yourself.

1.  Open a new browser tab or grab your smartphone and navigate to your live online store.
2.  Go to one of the products you enabled in Step 2.
3.  Ensure the new "Try-On" button appears where you placed it.
4.  Click the button. Upload a photo of yourself (or use a model photo) and test the generation process. Does the garment drape correctly? Is the speed satisfactory?

If it looks good to you, it's ready for your customers.

## Step 5: Consult Your Stats After a Day

The beauty of VTO isn't just the cool factor; it's the data.

Don't just set it and forget it. Come back to the Genlook dashboard after 24 hours of being live. In the **Analytics** section, look for early indicators of success:

* **Engagement Rate:** How many visitors on enabled product pages are actually clicking the "Try-On" button?
* **Sessions:** How many unique try-on sessions have been generated?

Seeing customers actively engage with the tool in the first 24 hours is the strongest signal that you are solving a real friction point in their shopping journey.
