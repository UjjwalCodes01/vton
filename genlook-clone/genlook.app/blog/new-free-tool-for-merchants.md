We're thrilled to launch a new, free tool designed to help fashion merchants create compelling marketing assets. You can now generate images of your products on a variety of models, perfect for social media, ads, and your website.

This tool is designed to help you create authentic, user-generated content (UGC) style images that resonate with modern shoppers.

## How to Use the Tool

1.  **Visit our Tools page:** You'll find the new "Virtual Try-On for Merchants" tool.
2.  **Upload your product image:** Provide a clear image of the garment you want to feature.
3.  **Choose a model:** Select from our diverse range of AI-generated models.
4.  **Generate your image:** Our AI will create a high-quality image of the model wearing your product.

This is a powerful way to showcase your products in a more relatable and engaging way, without the cost and complexity of a traditional photoshoot.

We're committed to providing value to the fashion e-commerce community, and we hope this free tool helps you grow your business.
