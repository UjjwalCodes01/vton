## What Is ChatGPT Shopping and Instant Checkout?

In late 2025 and early 2026, [OpenAI](https://openai.com/blog/buy-it-in-chatgpt), in partnership with Stripe, launched the **Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)**. This open-source standard allows AI agents to securely facilitate purchases directly within a chat interface. 

For the consumer, the experience is magical. They ask ChatGPT, *"Find me a sustainable red dress for a beach wedding under $200."* ChatGPT searches merchant feeds, presents the best options, and provides a "Buy Now" button right in the chat. The user completes the purchase without ever visiting the merchant's actual website.

Over 1 million Shopify merchants are now eligible for this feature, joining major early adopters like Glossier, SKIMS, and Spanx.

## How the Technical Flow Works Behind the Scenes

While the user experience feels like buying directly from OpenAI, you remain fully in control as the merchant. 

1. **Discovery:** ChatGPT reads your optimized product feed.
2. **Recommendation:** It presents your product to the user in the chat interface.
3. **Checkout:** The user clicks "Buy". The Agentic Commerce Protocol uses a Shared Payment Token (SPT) via Stripe to securely pass payment data.
4. **Fulfillment:** Your Shopify backend receives the order, calculates shipping and taxes, and accepts the payment. You fulfill the order exactly as you normally would.

## Step-by-Step: Enabling ChatGPT Shopping on Shopify

Getting your store technically ready for ChatGPT is surprisingly simple. Because Shopify has natively integrated with OpenAI, you do not need a developer to wire up custom APIs. You can generally manage this right from the [Shopify Admin](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-sales-channels).

1. **Enable the Channel:** Go to your Shopify Admin and install the [ChatGPT sales channel from the Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/).
2. **Opt-In:** Explicitly opt-in to the Instant Checkout feature. (See [Shopify's official documentation on managing Sales Channels](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-sales-channels) for step-by-step guidance).
3. **Payments:** Ensure your [Stripe payment integration](https://stripe.com/en-ca/newsroom/news/stripe-openai-instant-checkout) is active and properly configured for delegated payments under the Agentic Commerce Protocol.
4. **Register:** Sign up at [chatgpt.com/merchants](https://chatgpt.com/merchants) to join the OpenAI merchant program and ensure your product feed is set to real-time or daily sync.

## The Real Work: Optimizing Your Product Feed for AI Discovery

Technical setup is only step one. ChatGPT does not read your beautiful website layout; it reads your raw product data feed. If your data is messy, AI agents will ignore you. Data shows that stores with 99.9% attribute completion see **3-4x higher AI visibility**.

To optimize your feed:
*   **Conversational Titles:** Replace SKU-style naming conventions. Instead of `"W-Dress-Red-01"`, use `"Women's Sustainable Red Maxi Dress, Summer Beach Wedding, Lightweight"`.
*   **Complete All Attributes:** Fill in GTINs, colors, sizes, materials, and target demographics for *every single variant*. Do not leave fields blank.
*   **Rich Metafields:** Add structured data for care instructions, shipping times, and return windows.
*   **Enriched Descriptions:** Write descriptions that focus on *use cases* and *problems solved*. AI models reason over text, so telling the AI that a dress is "perfect for hot summer destination weddings" helps it match the product to specific user prompts.
