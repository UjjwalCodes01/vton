We're thrilled to expand Genlook's capabilities beyond apparel. You can now offer virtual try-on experiences for **footwear** and **eyewear**.

Until now, our specialized AI models were optimized primarily for clothing like tops, dresses, and jackets. With this latest update, we've integrated new, category-specific AI modules designed to handle the unique challenges of rigid accessories.

## What's New?

### 👟 Footwear Support
Whether you sell sneakers, boots, or formal shoes, Genlook can now visualize them on your customers' feet. The AI understands foot positioning and shoe structure to generate realistic try-on results.

### 👓 Eyewear Support
Selling glasses or sunglasses? Our new eyewear module accurately places frames on the user's face, handling transparency and fit with impressive precision.

## How to Enable It
No action is needed! Our system now automatically detects the product type (shoe, eyewear, or clothing) and routes it to the most appropriate AI model for the best results.

This update opens up virtual try-on to a whole new range of products, helping you reduce returns and boost confidence for every item in your catalog.
