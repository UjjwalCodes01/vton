Shopping is often a social experience, and we believe virtual try-on should be too. That's why we're excited to introduce our new **Sharing Feature**!

Your customers can now easily share their AI-generated virtual try-on looks with friends, family, or on social media.


## How It Works

When a customer generates a try-on image using the Genlook widget on your store, they will now see a "Share" button. 

Clicking this button generates a unique, shareable link. This link displays:
- The customer's virtual try-on result.
- A direct link back to the specific product page on your store.

## Why this is great for your store

1. **Increased Social Proof:** When customers share their looks, they are essentially endorsing your products to their network.
2. **Free Organic Traffic:** Every shared link includes a button directing viewers back to your product page, driving high-intent, organic traffic to your store.
3. **Higher Engagement:** Allowing users to get second opinions from friends can give them the confidence they need to complete their purchase.
