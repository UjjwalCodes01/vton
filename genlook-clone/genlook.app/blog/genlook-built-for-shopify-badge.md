We are thrilled to announce that Genlook has officially earned the **Built for Shopify** badge! 

This badge is Shopify's highest level of recognition for apps. It signifies that Genlook meets Shopify's strict standards for quality, design, performance, and security.

![Built for Shopify Badge](/blog-images/built-for-shopify-badge.png)

## What this means for our merchants

Earning the Built for Shopify badge isn't just a milestone for us; it's a guarantee for you. Here is what it means when you use Genlook:

- **Blazing Fast Performance:** Genlook is optimized to ensure it doesn't slow down your storefront. Your customers get a seamless virtual try-on experience without compromising your site's speed.
- **Native Look and Feel:** We've integrated deeply with Shopify's Polaris design system. Genlook feels like a natural extension of your Shopify admin, making it intuitive and easy to use.
- **Reliability and Security:** Meeting Shopify's highest standards means you can trust Genlook to handle your store's traffic and data securely.

We want to thank all our early adopters and merchants who have provided invaluable feedback. Your insights have driven our continuous improvements and helped us reach this milestone.
